About us

Who we are

Zukuka traders is a sacco with a sole aim of helping people out of poverty by providing a platform for saving .Zukuka also provides loans at low interest rates enabling people to start up businesses.

What we do

Zukuka provides loans and saving services to its customers . loan include:

  • Education loans.these are loans aimed at helping parents pay for school fees and tuition
  • Agricultural loans.these help farmers to invest in their produce
  • Fast loans.these are loans which require less paper work for emergencies
we also have numerous account types like:
  • Student account
  • Fixed saving account
  • Investment account
